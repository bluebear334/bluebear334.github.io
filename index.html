Who Is This Guy?

My name is Rikard Saqe and I'm a 16 year old obsessed with learning all I can about all I can while putting that knowledge towards good use. I'm trying my best to build as diverse of an exprience set as possible, exploring a broad set of topics, the most recent of which has been blockchain.

My past experience includes having programmed several decentralized applications, winning a few hackathons, and interning at Coinberry, one of Canada's leading crypto exchanges, in growth marketing, where we 5xed users in a matter of 2 months. I have also worked directly with the executive teams of several multimillion dollar blockchain projects to aid them in content creation in the form of podcasts, slide decks, articles, and infographics.
